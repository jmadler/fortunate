Fortunate: a modern fortune(1)
Fortunate is a modern application that aims to be a simple best-in-class, cross-platform sample. It comes with a bundle of fortune files -- lists of fortunes to show -- and allows users to add their own fortune files.
It's currently available as a Progressive Web App, and versions are planned for other platforms as well.
For any feedback, please reach out to me on GitHub.
